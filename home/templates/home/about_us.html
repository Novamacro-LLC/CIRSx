Our Purpose

  • To create and refine standards, while staying on the cutting edge and using an evidence-based approach.
  • To transform multiple industries and their approaches toward Chronic Inflammatory Response Syndrome (CIRS) and other environmentally related health challenges.
  • Work with well-known methods to test new technology for better outcomes.

Our Vision

  • To identify the environmental problems, identify knowledge gaps, contribute to and perform research, develop solutions and teach both the problems and solutions to the environmental and medical worlds.
  • To further educate other realms such as legal, layperson, medical and residential/commercial insurance worlds.
  • To create new standards and refine older standards by reviewing literature, professional experiences and collected data allowing the evidence to guide best practices.
  • To create a safe place for varying disciplines, such as environmental professionals, healthcare providers and others, to interface and interact to develop multi-industry solutions for environmental problems.
  • To develop a repository of trusted educational and research materials and make these resources available to the different worlds.
